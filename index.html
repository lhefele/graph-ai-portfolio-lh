About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher with expertise in the full spectrum of epidemiological investigations—from rigorous study design and digital data capture to advanced statistical modelling in R, Stata and Python. My portfolio includes serological surveys, infectious disease research and reproducible analytics pipelines, a subset of which are openly shared on GitHub. Passionate about open science, I advocate version‑controlled workflows and transparent reporting. I also leverage generative AI to accelerate literature reviews, support code development, automate routine tasks and create presentations.
